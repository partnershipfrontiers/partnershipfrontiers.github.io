Business Training Seminars


Seminars are available to provide both for-profit and not-for profit organizations with valuable concepts for profitable growth and practical plans for goal implementation.  Topics include:


  1. •For-profit lessons for the not-for-profit sector

  2. •Contrasting sales strategies for Canadian and US companies

  3. •Understanding the Quebec culture and business climates


Meeting facilitation, one-on-one lessons and individualized workshops are also available.



Community / School Educational Programs


Mixing history and culture with humor provides participants of all ages with fun-filled enrichment opportunities. 


Popular topics for school / community groups include:


  1. •Beyond Freedom Fries: French influence in North America

  2. •Quebec: Living French culture without crossing an ocean

  3. •C’est What?  Understanding Quebec French

  4. •French in the workplace: It isn’t just for teachers anymore!


Educators can take advantage of targeted staff development workshops on:

  1. •French teaching resources (on a budget!)

  2. •Teaching Quebec French in an historical context

  3. •Business French for the secondary classroom
