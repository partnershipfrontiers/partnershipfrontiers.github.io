Partnership Frontiers LLC is a bilingual (English-French) consulting firm offering relationship development assistance through targeted marketing strategies, event planning support and general management services. Specializing in US/Canadian relations, the firm works domestically and internationally across business sectors to assist clients to create profitable, effective and complementary partnerships in both trade and cultural arenas. 


Partnership Frontiers LLC also offers experienced presenters and trainers to convey messaging to a wide variety of business, community and school audiences.
