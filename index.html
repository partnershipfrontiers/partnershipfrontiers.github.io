Specializing in US/Canadian relations, the firm works domestically and internationally across business sectors to assist clients to create profitable, effective and complementary partnerships in both trade and cultural arenas.

Partnership Frontiers LLC also offers experienced presenters and trainers to convey messaging to a wide variety of business, community and school audiences.

Targeted Marketing Strategies

Public relations and marketing presents a challenge to many companies, particularly small operations. The costs of purchasing advertising among a wealth of sources and the keen competition among providers can seem to be an overwhelming proposition.

By identifying and collaborating with the right partners, small organizations can leverage their public relations dollars to yield greater marketing success in a cost-effective way.

Partnership Frontiers LLC recognizes the increasing importance of corporate social responsibility in the North American marketplace. Wherever possible, the firm attempts to connect clients’ public relations initiatives with community investment. By establishing a perception that they are a “corporate good citizen” companies can more effectively penetrate new markets while motivating employees to contribute to local communities.

Event Planning Support

Whether planning a trade mission or a community arts program, Partnership Frontiers LLC can help you to make sure the end product is of the highest quality. Working with you, we can oversee any or all of the following aspects of your program.

  • Developing pertinent, compelling event theme
  • Qualifying participants
  • Publicizing and overseeing registration
  • Securing venues and catering
  • Facilitating event
  • Providing follow-up communications
